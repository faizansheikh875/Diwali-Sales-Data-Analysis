# Diwali Sales Analysis 

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Diwali sales data to understand customer buying behavior 
based on Gender, Age Group, Marital Status, Occupation, and Product Category.


## Libraries Used
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

- 
## Key Insights
- Married women are more likely to buy compared to unmarried and even men
- top 3 state like UP, MAHARASTRA, KARNATAKA
- top product Categories like Food,cloth, electronics 
- Top Occupation = IT, HealthCare, aviation
- 26-36 age group women more likly to buy comapared to other age group women and men\

- 
## CONCLUSION
Company should focus on married female customers aged 26–35 years in the states of Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, as their order count is the highest.
The top three occupations contributing to sales are Health, IT, and Aviation.
The top three product categories generating maximum orders are Clothing, Food, and Electronics.
